Meshcon

10-15 Oct. 2014 Berlin

  • GitHub Profile

MeshCon Fashion & Tec Week Berlin 2014 is the first conference of its kind bringing together fashion makers, designers, pattern creators, knitters, textile manipulators, programmers, diy and fun loving people in Berlin. The event offers a place to learn about new ideas, upcoming technologies in the garment and textile area and start ups.

Berlin is a great and creative city and the event will take place in the center of the city.
