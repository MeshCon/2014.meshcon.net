MeshCon Fashion & Tec Week Berlin 2014

MeshCon Berlin is the first conference of its kind bringing together fashion makers, designers, pattern creators, knitters, textile manipulators, programmers, diy and fun loving people in Berlin. The event offers a place to learn about new ideas, upcoming technologies in the garment and textile area and start ups.

Berlin is a great and creative city and the event will take place in the center of the city at the University of Technology TU Berlin and the Wikimedia Deutschland e.V. representative office from October 10-15 2014.
