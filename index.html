MeshCon Fashion & Tec Week Berlin 2014

The first edition of MeshCon is taking place in Berlin from October 10-15, 2014. The topic of the event is how to reap the benefits of participatory production in the textile industry. The MeshCon Berlin brings together scientists, fashion makers, designers, pattern creators, knitters, textile manipulators, programmers and DIY hardware makers. The event offers a place to learn about new ideas in fashion, upcoming technologies in the garment and textile area and contribute to Open Knowledge.

Participants from across the world are expected to join the five day event at locations around Berlin city. The first day will start with a conference at the University of Technology TU Berlin. In the following days the event will offer workshops at the Wikimedia Deutschland e.V. representative office. Topics are fashion design and cutting-edge technologies, using software fashion tools and developing your own wearables.
